How a Thinking Skill works
💡
You start with an idea or goal
"I want to build a neighborhood tool-sharing app"
🔍
AI researches the domain
Web searches, market data, expert advice, real examples
🖼️
Shows you a visual decision page
4 options with previews, comparison table, and a recommendation
🧑
You make the call
AI recommends, but you decide. Your judgment shapes everything downstream.
💾
Decision remembered
Stored in .decisions/ as a browsable HTML page you can revisit anytime
📋
Strategy brief with all your decisions
A complete record of what you decided and why, ready for the next skill
